FlappyBird

...the most popular mobile game in 2014

Gameplay

  • Flappy Bird is an arcade-style game in which you control the bird Faby, which moves persistently to the right.

  • Your task is to navigate Faby through pairs of pipes and try to reach new high scores.

  • Colliding with a pipe or the ground ends the gameplay.

Development

  • Flappy Bird clone was developed as a submission to the Mintbean Hiring Hackathon

  • Built entirely with Phaser3 game framework
